#BlackLivesMatter

The SEBS Scholarship Foundation stands with the Black community in the fight against racism and injustice.

White supremacy and police brutality affect millions of individuals worldwide. Therefore, we must come together worldwide to join in the fight!


How We Combat Discrimination

In order to provide every SEBS graduate a fair and equal chance at receiving a scholarship award, we adopted a non-discriminatory statement on November 19, 2019 that you can find in the footer of every page on our site. We enforce this statement in our application review process by having our security officers anonymize each application. Award recipients must be voted on by the entire selection committee to ensure that the decision isn't made by only a few individuals.

How You Can Help

As a small non-profit, we have very limited resources that we are required by law to use for our charitable purpose of providing scholarships (and we have to keep our promise to our amazing donors). Because we aren't able to directly make a contribution, we'd like to encourage everyone to visit the website below for resources and information on how to make an impact.
