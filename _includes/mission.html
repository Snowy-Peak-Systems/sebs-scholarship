Mission Statement

The mission of the SEBS Scholarship Foundation is to assist in paying the costs of post-secondary educational opportunities for graduating students of the School of Engineering and Biomedical Science at Pueblo County High School by awarding annual SEBS Scholarships. Our aim is to help those students who have displayed high academic achievement, work ethic, and a commitment to helping their community and improving themselves through furthering their education.

Who We Are

Founded by the SEBS class of 2017, the SEBS Scholarship Foundation is a non-profit public charity dedicated to helping SEBS students afford to further their education. We do this by providing yearly scholarships to graduating SEBS seniors who demonstrate characteristics that align with our core values.

We aim to benefit students who demonstrate the following characteristics:

  • Persist in the face of challenges, both academic and personal.
  • Desire to improve the world around them.
  • Maintain an uncompromising character that demonstrates strength, respect, and honesty.
  • Demonstrate leadership potential and emotional maturity.
  • Shows enthusiasm in learning and takes initiative in one's own education.
