Eligibility Requirements


The minimum requirements needed to apply for this scholarship are as follows:

  • Be a currently enrolled senior graduating from the School of Engineering and Biomedical Science at Pueblo County High School.

  • Hold a cumulative weighted GPA of at least 3.0 at the time of application.

  • Student must enroll in an accredited 2- or 4-year degree program in the fall semester of their graduation year.

Application Requirements


The application includes two written responses and your high school transcript. Listed below are all the required parts of the application.

  • Upload your latest unofficial high school transcript as a single PDF document.

  • Briefly describe a problem related to a field you're interested in. Then, please address the following in detail: What actions can individuals in this industry take to solve the problem? Response must be 500 words or fewer.

  • Review the values of the SEBS Scholarship Foundation listed below. Choose one of these values and describe a time when you learned the importance of this value. Response must be 500 words or fewer.
