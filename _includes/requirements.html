Eligibility Requirements


The minimum requirements needed to apply for this scholarship are as follows:

  • Be a currently enrolled senior graduating from the School of Engineering and Biomedical Science at Pueblo County High School.

  • Student must enroll in an accredited 2- or 4-year degree program in the fall semester of their graduation year.

Application Requirements


The application consists of two written responses listed below.

  • Briefly describe a problem related to a field you're interested in. Then, in detail, describe the most promising solution(s) and why they stand out. Address limitations or counterarguments. Response must be 500 words or less.

  • Think of one extracurricular activity that has inspired you. Tell the story of a meaningful lesson you learned through your involvement in this activity. Response must be 500 words or less.
