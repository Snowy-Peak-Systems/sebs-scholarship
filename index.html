Eligibility Requirements


The minimum requirements needed to apply for this scholarship are as follows:

  • Be a currently enrolled senior graduating from the School of Engineering and Biomedical Sciences at Pueblo County High School.

  • Hold a cumulative GPA of at least 3.0 at the time of application.

  • Earn a minimum SAT Math score of 470 and a minimum Evidence-Based Reading & Writing score of 450, or a minimum ACT score of 20 in each category (writing score not required). All scores must come from a single test; super-scoring is not accepted.

  • Individual demonstrates characteristics that align with scholarship core values:

    • Academic performance or promise.

    • Uncompromising character that demonstrates strength, respect, and honesty.

    • Leadership potential, emotional maturity and stability.

    • Well-rounded personality, abilities, and broad interests.

    • Desire to contribute to oneâ€™s community.

Application Requirements


The application is comprised of a form with three additional supporting documents. Listed below are all the required parts of the application. Submit the application and all of its parts by the deadline in order to be considered.

  • Complete and submit the online application.

    • As part of this application, you will be asked to write a response to a provided prompt in no more than 500 words.

  • Upload your latest unofficial high school transcript as a single PDF document.

  • Upload a signed letter of recommendation from a professional reference as a single PDF document. SEBS faculty members CANNOT write this letter.

  • Create and upload a portfolio that explains and/or demonstrates, in no more than 500 words, a significant project or achievement that occurred during your time at SEBS as a single PDF document.

    • Be sure to include a description of the project and the issue it addresses, the project's requirements, and any supplemental graphics that you feel may be beneficial to provide.

  • Optionally, you can upload a cover letter of no more than 500 words if you feel that there is any additional information that may help us in reviewing your application. There is no penalty for not submitting a cover letter.
